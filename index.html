About halfie's LTD

HALFIE'S LTD is a service providing company founded by Guilaume Rurangwa Munyemana in 2019. The company offers both short term and long term services to help business, companies, or organizations in management, problems solving, and archieve more benefits from their works. We strive to make our clients appreciate the quality of our work

Our Staff comprise of full time and part time employees, and interns who works for a short duration of time. Our Staff are committed to working hard to make their partners happy with the services that we provide. Despite their individual qualifications, all our staff goes through an intense training in order for them to be fully equip with the necessary skills requird to deliver quality services
